ADAM // COMMAND CENTER — SNIPER SCOPE

Three feels of the down-the-scope game · LEFT stick = pan aim · ZOOM slider/wheel/pinch · RIGHT = fire · drag & hold to play

01 · RIDGE HUNTER02 · VALLEY RUN03 · DEEP SCOPE
01RIDGE HUNTERpop-up whack-a-mole — catch the peek before it ducks
Agents peek from behind a layered ridgeline for a beat, then drop. Left stick pans, zoom in on the right, hold FIRE to snipe.

Ridge Hunter: a green-phosphor CRT sniper-scope mockup. Look down a mil-dot scope at a parallax mountain ridgeline and snipe enemy agents that pop up from cover. Drag the left joystick to pan your aim, work the right-edge zoom, and press-hold the FIRE button to shoot.

02VALLEY RUNmoving targets — lead the runners across the valley
Agents sprint across open ground between cover. Track them with the left stick, work the zoom, hold FIRE; blow the fuel drums for chains.

Valley Run: a green-phosphor CRT sniper-scope game mockup. Look down a rifle scope at a wide valley and shoot agents sprinting across open ground. Drag the left joystick to pan your aim, work the zoom slider on the right to magnify, and press-hold the right FIRE button to shoot.

03DEEP SCOPEextreme long range — heavy 2x-14x zoom, hold your breath
Tiny far-off agents across hazy ranges; you NEED the zoom to see them. Pronounced sway, big long-range pops, distant depot chain blasts.

Deep Scope: an interactive green-phosphor CRT sniper-scope mockup. Look down a mil-dot rifle scope at distant hazy mountain ridgelines and pick off tiny enemy agents. Drag the bottom-left joystick to pan your aim, use the zoom control to magnify from 2x to 14x, and press-and-hold the bottom-right fire button to shoot.
